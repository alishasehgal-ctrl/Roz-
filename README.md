Roz — A Consistency App for Real Days

Most people do not struggle with motivation. They struggle with recovery. One slow morning, one missed workout, one bad meal — and the whole day feels like a write-off. Roz is built to fix that.

Roz is a consistency app that helps you make the rest of today count. It looks at where you are right now — how much day is left, your energy, what still matters — and gives you the next best step to keep moving forward. Not tomorrow. Not Monday. Right now.

It focuses on the small things that shape a real day: movement, water, protein, and a simple plan for tonight. No streaks to protect, no guilt when things slip, no starting over. Just accountability to yourself and a calm nudge to keep going.
